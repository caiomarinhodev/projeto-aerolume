About us

AEROLUME is a research group on Physics and Chemistry of the upper atmosphere. The main researches include dynamics and structures of the equatorial mesosphere and upper thermosphere by using airglow measurements and radio wave technique. AEROLUME is part of the Physics department of the UFCG.
